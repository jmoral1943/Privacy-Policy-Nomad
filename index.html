Privacy Policy for Nomad: Itinerary Planner

Effective date: 08/29/23

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nomad: Itinerary Planner by Graphite dev LLC. We respect your privacy and want to protect your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your personal data.

2. Data Collection

We collect personal email addresses to use for authentication purposes. In the future, we might collect additional personal information, but we will seek your consent when or if that happens.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We use the data collected for account creation, marketing, and to enhance app functionality.

4. Cookies

While we do not currently use cookies, we reserve the right to use them in the future to enhance user experience and functionality.

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We utilize third-party services such as Firebase to store data. We share crash reports, authentication data, and user preferences to improve app recommendations. Data like activity preferences may be used to offer better suggestions to other users.

6. Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your personal data from our systems, please email us at info@graphitedev.com, and we will assist you in the process.

7. Data Storage

We store data in Firebase's cloud database and locally on users' devices for quicker access.

8. Data Security

We value your data's security and use encryption methods to ensure its safety. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@graphitedev.com.

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting on the website.
